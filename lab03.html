BLUE COLOR

It is a color between violet and green in the light spectrum, and it is classified within the cold colors, and the blue color is a popular color because it is the color of nature, i.e. the color of the sky, seas, and oceans, and there are many shades of blue, including turquoise, turquoise, light blue, sky blue, and dark blue, And night blue and other degrees.

Indications of the blue color:

  • Blue is the color of the sky and the sea, and it is associated with open spaces, freedom, imagination, expansion, inspiration, and sensitivity. It indicates a love for life and vast spaces.
  • Blue represents meanings including depth, trust, loyalty, sincerity, wisdom, confidence, stability, faith and intelligence.
  • It has a positive effect on the mind and body, and as the color of the soul, it invokes comfort and can cause the body to produce chemicals that calm down and spread feelings of serenity
